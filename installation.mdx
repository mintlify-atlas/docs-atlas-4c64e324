title: "Install ByeTunes on your iPhone via sideloading"
description: "Download the ByeTunes IPA and install it using AltStore, Signulous, or Sideloadly. Also covers installing the required LocalDevVPN companion app."
---

ByeTunes is not available on the App Store. It is distributed as a free IPA file that you install by sideloading — signing the app outside of Apple's standard distribution channel. This page covers the three most common methods and what to install alongside ByeTunes to make it work.

<Note>
  ByeTunes requires **iOS 16.0 or later**. Verify your iOS version in **Settings → General → About** before installing.
</Note>

## Before you start

You need two things on your device:

- **ByeTunes** — the app itself, installed via one of the methods below.
- **LocalDevVPN** — a separate app that creates the local tunnel ByeTunes uses to communicate with the iOS media database. Install it from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/localdevvpn/id6755608044) or AltStore PAL.

## Download the ByeTunes IPA

All releases are published on GitHub. Download the latest `.ipa` file from the [ByeTunes releases page](https://github.com/EduAlexxis/ByeTunes/releases).

Save the IPA somewhere accessible from your computer — you will use it in the steps below.

## Install ByeTunes

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="AltStore">
    AltStore signs apps using your Apple ID and refreshes them every 7 days. AltStore PAL (EU) removes the 7-day limit.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Install AltStore on your computer">
        Download AltStore from [altstore.io](https://altstore.io) and follow the setup guide to install the AltStore companion app on your Mac or PC and the AltStore app on your iPhone.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Open AltStore on your iPhone">
        Connect your iPhone to your computer via USB. Make sure AltStore's companion app is running on your computer.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Sideload the IPA">
        In AltStore on your iPhone, tap the **+** button in the **My Apps** tab, navigate to the ByeTunes `.ipa` file, and select it.

        AltStore will sign the IPA with your Apple ID and install it.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Trust the developer certificate">
        Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find your Apple ID under Developer App, and tap **Trust**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      If you use AltStore PAL, you can add the ByeTunes source directly if one is available, which automates future updates.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Signulous">
    Signulous is a paid signing service that installs apps over the air without needing a computer for each install.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Create a Signulous account">
        Sign up at [signulous.com](https://www.signulous.com) and subscribe to a plan. Signulous provides enterprise certificates that allow installation without per-device registration.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Upload the IPA">
        Log into the Signulous dashboard, go to **Upload IPA**, and upload the ByeTunes `.ipa` file you downloaded from GitHub.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Install on your device">
        After the IPA is processed, Signulous generates an install link. Open it on your iPhone in Safari and tap **Install**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Trust the certificate">
        Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find the enterprise certificate, and tap **Trust**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Warning>
      Enterprise certificates used by signing services can be revoked by Apple. If ByeTunes stops launching, you may need to re-install using a fresh certificate.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Sideloadly">
    Sideloadly is a free desktop app for Windows and macOS that signs and installs IPAs using your Apple ID.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Download and install Sideloadly">
        Get Sideloadly from [sideloadly.io](https://sideloadly.io) and install it on your Mac or Windows PC.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Connect your iPhone">
        Connect your iPhone to your computer via USB and unlock it. Accept the "Trust this Computer" prompt if it appears.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Load the IPA and sign">
        Open Sideloadly. Drag the ByeTunes `.ipa` file into the window, enter your Apple ID, and click **Start**. Sideloadly will sign the app and install it.

        Free Apple IDs are limited to 3 sideloaded apps at a time and require re-signing every 7 days.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Trust the developer certificate">
        Go to **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**, find your Apple ID under Developer App, and tap **Trust**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Install LocalDevVPN

LocalDevVPN is required for ByeTunes to function. It is available on the App Store — no sideloading needed.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download LocalDevVPN">
    Install [LocalDevVPN](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/localdevvpn/id6755608044) from the App Store. It is also available on AltStore PAL.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect the VPN">
    Open LocalDevVPN and tap **Connect**. Allow the VPN configuration when iOS prompts you.

    Keep LocalDevVPN connected any time you use ByeTunes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify your installation

Open ByeTunes. You should see the onboarding screen asking you to import a pairing file. If the app launches to this screen, installation was successful.

If ByeTunes crashes or shows an "Untrusted Developer" error, make sure you completed the certificate trust step in **Settings → General → VPN & Device Management**.
